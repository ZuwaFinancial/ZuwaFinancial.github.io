Quick Start Program

A one-time or as needed service designed to help those seeking to gain quick clarity on 2 to 3 financial topics most pressing to them.

Ideal For

  • A Do-It-Yourselfer seeking an expert 1st or 2nd opinion from a CFP
  • Anyone not quite ready for on-going goal-based financial planning service
  • nyone with one or two areas of financial concerns

What’s included?

  • A Do-It-Yourselfer seeking an expert 1st or 2nd opinion from a CFP
  • A 60-90 minutes collaborative session in-person (Dallas office) or virtually (via video conference) to discuss most urgent financial questions
  • Working session will be augmented by behind-the-scenes time to be spent preparing, analyzing, and developing recommendations for the client to implement
  • Financial Planning topics includes Net worth, Budgeting, cash reserves, cash flow management, college education planning, retirement planning, Income Tax Planning, and Insurance Review
  • Two months of email support to issues addressed
  • One-time fee starting at $200 ($100 per hour with a 2-hour minimum)
  • Fees are billed at completion of the session. If a client chooses to engage Zuwa Financial for Comprehensive Financial Planning within two months of completion of a Quick Start program, any fees paid may be credited towards the Comprehensive Financial Planning fee.

**The Quick Start Program does not include asset allocation, investment risk profiling or investment planning. Please see the Comprehensive Planning Program if you desire those services at this time. **

Financial Foundations Program

Designed to help our clients build a solid foundation upon which their long-term wealth will be based. Building wealth over time that will last a lifetime requires discipline and smart planning. While cash-flow planning, budgeting, and establishing an emergency fund might seem trivial, these elements comprise the bedrock of a great financial plan.

Ideal for:

  • “Beginners” to financial planning (Individuals and couples)
  • Anyone feeling overwhelmed by expenses and/or debt
  • Anyone not generating enough income to invest outside of their employer retirement plans

What’s included?

  • A 60-90 minutes session in-person (Dallas office) or virtually (via video conference)
  • A 60-min plan delivery session in-person or virtually
  • A 30-min check-in meeting every 4 months via phone or virtually
  • Working sessions will be augmented by behind-the-scenes time to be spent preparing, analyzing, and developing recommendations for the client to implement
  • Financial planning topics includes net worth, budgeting, cash reserves, cash flow management, college education planning, retirement planning, income tax planning, and insurance review
  • One-time fee starting at $298 ($149 per hour with a 2-hour minimum)
  • Fees are billed at completion of the session. If a client chooses to engage Zuwa Financial for Comprehensive Financial Planning within two months of completion of a Financial Foundation Program, any fees paid may be credited towards the Comprehensive Financial Planning fee.

Comprehensive Financial Planning

Designed for people who have a variety of financial concerns and would like to start proactively planning for a prosperous financial future. You desire to work with a tech-savvy planner CFP® who stays current on the financial planning strategies that meet your needs, goals and complex financial situations on an ongoing basis.

Ideal for:

  • Individuals, Couples, and families entering/planning for a new life-stage or event
  • Anyone generating enough income to pursue multiple financial goals at the same time
  • Anyone seeking access and a long-term relationship with a financial advisor who can advise on many topics on an unlimited basis

What’s included?

  • A 90-min intake session in-person (NYC office) or virtually (via video conference)
  • A 90-min plan delivery session in-person or virtually
  • Assistance & guidance in implementing the financial plan
  • Three personalized 60-min coaching and progress-check sessions
  • Update, monitor, and modify your plan as needed plus unlimited email or phone support for questions that arise throughout
  • Working sessions will be augmented by behind-the-scenes time spent preparing, analyzing, and developing recommendations for the client
  • Financial planning topics includes net worth, budgeting, cash reserves, cash flow management, college education planning, retirement planning, income tax planning, insurance review, investment planning, estate planning, vision, value, and goals. Investment Management is an ADD ON service on this program.
  • Initial fee starting at $300 and annual retainer of $70 - $400 charged monthly depending on the complexity of the plan

Other Uniquely Designed Services Include

  • Discovery Meeting: A 60-minute meeting where we gain clarity on your values, financial history, and financial obstacles
  • Comprehensive Financial Planning Analysis: A review of your entire financial picture that includes an overview of your values and goals, net worth statement, cash flow analysis, recommendations in the applicable areas below, and itemized action plan
  • Plan Delivery Meeting: A 90-minute meeting to deliver Customized Recommendations, and Action Plan
  • Implementation: Assistance implementing action plan items and recommendations, communications, and coordination with other financial professionals
  • Semi-Annual Review Meetings: A 60-minute meeting every 6 months to measure your progress, make adjustments, and identify new action steps
