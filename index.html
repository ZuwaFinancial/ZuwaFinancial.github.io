About Me

Hello, I am Richard Omobogie.
I am a Certified Financial Planner® and have worked with several nationally recognized firms over the last eight years.

I started Zuwa Financial to help families and small buisinesses reach their financial goals.

My goal is to guide people toward make smart financial choices through both the pleasant and the stressful experiences that life brings.
