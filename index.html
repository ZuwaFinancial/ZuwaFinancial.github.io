About Me

Hello, I am Richard Omobogie.
I am a Certified Financial Planner® and have worked with several nationally recognized firms over the last eight years.

I started Zuwa Financial to help families and small businesses reach their financial goals.

Dallas, Texas is now my home, but I come from humble beginnings in Nigeria.

I named my firm Zuwa, which means "Chosen to provide wealth and prosperity." I take my firm's name seriously and work hard to be an exceptional resource for my clients.

My goal is to guide people toward making smart financial choices through both the pleasant and the stressful experiences that life brings.
