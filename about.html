ABOUT

Richard Omobogie is a Certified Financial Planner and a Chartered Retirement Planning Counselor who has worked at several nationally recognized firms over the last eight years.

His background with companies such as Ameriprise Financial and Wells Fargo Advisors has provided him with a wide range of experience in the field.

Richard is the founder and CEO of Zuwa Financial LLC, an independent financial advisory practice, where he provides FEE ONLY financial planning and investment management services.

This “Zero Sales” model of financial consulting allows him to focus on the area he is most passionate about: planning and advice for individuals and families regardless of their wealth or circumstances.

Richard prides himself on being a joyful and energetic part of the community. He has been called upon by various professional associations and ethnic organizations to speak publically about different financial topics.

These pro bono presentations are a way of doing educational outreach in order to help individuals in all walks of life become more educated and able to make smart financial decisions for themselves and their families.

He also supports charitable organizations and sporting clubs in the Dallas/Forth Worth metroplex.

Richard is originally from Nigeria but currently lives with his wife and 3 daughters in Dallas, Texas.
