ABOUT

I am Richard Omobogie.

I am the founder and CEO of Zuwa Financial LLC, an independent financial advisory practice.

My firm provides fee-only financial planning and investment management services.

My background with companies such as Ameriprise Financial and Wells Fargo Advisors has provided me with a wide range of experience in financial services.

As a fee-only financial planner, I have no sales goals to meet, unlike traditional financial advisors.
This allows my to take a "zero sales" approach when offering my financial consulting services.
Instead, I can focus on the areas I am most passionate about: planning and advice for individuals and families--regardless of their wealth or circumstances.

I pride myself on being a joyful and energetic part of the community.
I work with professional associations and ethnic organizations to increase financial literacy in the Dallas community.

Although I am originally from Nigeria, I currently live in Dallas, Texas.
